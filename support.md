# GeoScratch Support

Thanks for using GeoScratch! If you need help, have feedback, or want to report a problem, please contact us.

## Contact

Email: **support@koneckta.com**

When contacting support, please include:

- Your device model
- Your iOS or iPadOS version
- A brief description of the issue
- A screenshot, if possible

## Frequently Asked Questions

### How do I reveal a fact?

Use your finger to scratch the card until the country and its fact are revealed.

### How does the daily streak work?

Open GeoScratch and complete the daily scratch card to continue building your streak.

### Why isn't the scratch card responding?

Close and reopen GeoScratch. If the issue continues, restart your device and confirm that you're using the latest version of the app.

### How do I share a fact?

After revealing a fact, tap **Share this fact** and select one of the sharing options shown by your device.
